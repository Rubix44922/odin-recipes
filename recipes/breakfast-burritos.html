Breakfast Burritos

Home

A breakfst burrito consists of tortillas, potatoes, bacon, ground beef, and eggs.

They can be easily prepared in bulk and reheated to be eaten on the go.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Potatoes
  • Bacon
  • Ground Beef
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin

Steps

  1. Prepare the potatoes
    1. Dice the potatoes
    2. Parboil for 5-6 minutes
    3. Season the potatoes
    4. Bake at 425° for 25-30 minutes
  2. Fry bacon
  3. Fry ground beef
  4. Scramble eggs
  5. Assemble on toritillas
  6. Sear and wrap
